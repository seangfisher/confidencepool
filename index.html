Nick's Confidence Pool

How to play

Select the winner of each game and then assign a confidence number to that game, with 1 being the least confident and 16 (if there are no teams on bye) being the most confident. If you correctly pick the winner, you get the associated points, otherwise you get 0 for that game.

Whoever has the highest points for the week gets the pot for that week. If there is a tie in the points, it comes down to the tiebreaker. For the tiebreaker, enter your guess at the COMBINED score of the Monday night game. (i.e. if you think it will be 13-24 you would enter 37)

Rules

$2 entry fee each week.

The Thursday night pick has to be in by 7:20pm CST each Thursday. Your other picks do not need to be submitted at this time. You can just text/email me your Thursday pick and then email me everything else later.

All other picks must be submitted by the first kickoff on Sunday. This will typically be noon, but if there is an international game, it could be earlier.
